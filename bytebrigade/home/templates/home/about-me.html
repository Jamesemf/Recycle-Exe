About Me

Rec-Ex is a tool to help and encourage students to recycle on campus. It's main features are:

  • To navigate students to the nearest recycling points around campus
  • To reward students for recycling by awarding them points and ranking them on a leaderboard
  • To allow students to create and track goals for themselves that they aspire to achieve
  • To let students view each other's profiles and see how their recycling compares with others

How does it work?

All you have to do is scan the barcode of the product that you would like to recycle and it will use your location to detect the recycling bin that you're nearest to. You can also view the feed to view which items have been recently recycled by other students. 

You can then register the item that you're recycling to gain points and potentially increase your leaderboard ranking.
Select the leaderboard tab in the navbar to view your current position on the leaderboard. 


You can also access other features through your profile page which can be accessed using the navbar at the top of the webpage. Here you can view statistics such as your points, carbon footprint, and the weight in kg that you have recycled.

 You may also use the profile page to assign yourself recycling goals that we will keep track of for you to help reach your sustainability aspirations.
